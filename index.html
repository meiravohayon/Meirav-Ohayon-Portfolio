About

1999, BFA, Bezalel Academy of Art and Design, Jerusalem
2019, M.Des, Integrated Design, HIT - Holon Institute of Technology

My work moves between the figurative and the abstract, built from photographs that I rework through charcoal, acrylic, oil, and graphite on large-scale canvas. I draw on techniques from the world of textile - dyeing and washing the canvas before and after painting.

I'm drawn to charged, quiet states - moments that hold tension beneath a still surface. I don't set out to soften what's difficult; I try to give it a form that can be looked at.
